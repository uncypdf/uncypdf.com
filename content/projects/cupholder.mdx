# cupholder

![cupholder main](/collection/cupholder/01.png)

A hardwood cupholder designed as a small object for daily use.

## Overview

Designed for cups, bottles, and everyday objects, the cupholder works as a compact furniture accessory for desks, shelves, and low tables.

The form is simple and direct, but the object is built around the material contrast between wood and metal.

## Material

- Hardwood
- Aluminum

## Details

The body is shaped as a solid wooden volume, then refined through cutting, sanding, fitting, and finishing.

The aluminum detail adds a precise industrial contrast to the natural weight and surface of the wood.
