# cupholder

cupholder

A wooden cupholder object built around the contrast between walnut and aluminum.

## Material

- Walnut wood
- Aluminum

## Function

The object is designed as a small furniture accessory for holding cups, bottles, and everyday objects.

## Process

detail

The form begins as a simple cylindrical volume and is refined through cutting, sanding, fitting, and finishing.

## Notes

A compact object for everyday use, balancing utility and sculptural presence.
